Urban Dream

An AR project that recreats individuals’ experiences as digital urban landscapes.

The idea of “Social Sculpture” comes from Joseph Beuys’ most famous quote, “Everyone is an artist”, and is based around the notion of creating sculptures out of society, “Everyone” is “reshaping” or “remaking” the world form the physical to the virtual.

Urban dreams & story are kinds of city products, it exists every corner, it builds the city. I want to be an “urban Flâneur” to collect every “artists’” dreams and stories, just like Joseph Beuys said in Extended Concept of Art, the activity of Flâneur represents a sculptural process, shaping what he sees and listens in the environment and social appearance into a sculpture.
